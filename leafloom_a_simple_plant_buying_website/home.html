Got Questions?

How do you ensure plants arrive healthy? ↓

We use specially designed breathable eco-packaging that secures the pot and protects the leaves during transit.

What is your return policy? ↓

If your plant arrives damaged or dies within 14 days, we send a replacement free of charge. No questions asked.

Do you offer bulk discounts for offices? ↓

Yes! Contact our corporate team for customized greening solutions for your workspace.

Can I change or cancel my order? ↓

Orders can be modified or canceled within 2 hours of placement. Please contact our support team immediately.

Do you provide care instructions? ↓

Yes, every plant comes with a care card that includes watering, sunlight, and maintenance tips to help your plant thrive.

Are your plants pet-friendly? ↓

Some of our plants are pet-friendly, while others may be toxic to pets. Please check the product description for each plant's safety information.

How long does delivery take? ↓

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days, while express options are available for faster delivery. Delivery times may vary based on location.
