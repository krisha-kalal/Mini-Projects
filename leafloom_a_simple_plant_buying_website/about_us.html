Bred from a deep passion for thoughtful, modern spaces.

Found in 2021, Leafloom began inside a simple urban greenhouse out of a clear observation: modern apartments are missing organic warmth, and most industrial plant outlets sell items unsuited for indoor conditions.

We do things differently. We don't just ship plants; we select specimens based on structure, acclimate them meticulously over several weeks, and match them with custom-designed porous ceramic vessels.
